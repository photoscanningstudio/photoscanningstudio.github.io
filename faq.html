Frequently Asked Questions

How long does scanning take?

Typically 5–10 business days at our Hamburg location. Mail-in orders add shipping time—expect around 2 weeks total.

What types of media do you scan?

We scan all types of prints, Polaroids, and large-format photos up to 8.5" x 11" — all at no extra charge. Each includes color correction and enhancement.

What resolution do you use?

All scans are high-resolution at 600 DPI to preserve detail—you’ll get both original and enhanced copies.

Do you return my original photos?

Yes! Originals are carefully returned via mail or local pickup once scanning is complete.

How will I receive my digitized photos?

Choose between a USB ($20) or secure cloud delivery (FREE). Mail-in orders also include a download link.

Can I drop off my photos locally?

Yes! We provide free pickup/drop-off in Hamburg and nearby Western NY communities. Or you can drop off by appointment.
